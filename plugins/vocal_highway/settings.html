How It Works

  • Blue tubes show the target pitch and timing
  • Sing into your microphone -- the orange dot tracks your pitch
  • Tubes turn green when your pitch matches (+/-1 semitone)
  • Tubes turn red when off-pitch
  • Lyrics scroll across the top of the screen
  • The vocal stem is auto-muted so you sing the part yourself

Tips

  • Use headphones to prevent audio feedback into the mic
  • Disable browser noise suppression in audio settings for cleaner pitch detection
  • Octave equivalence: singing an octave higher or lower still counts as a hit
